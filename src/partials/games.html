Our Worlds

At LogicVia Games, we are passionate about creating worlds that players can explore, discover, and conquer. Our portfolio of games spans different genres, from action-adventure to strategic simulations. Here's a glimpse into the worlds we've created.

  • Chrono Rift: The Awakening

    Genre

    Action-Adventure, Time Manipulation

    Platform

    PC / PlayStation / Xbox

    Description

    Chrono Rift: The Awakening is a time-bending adventure where you play as a hero who must travel through multiple historical periods to stop an impending catastrophe. Solve puzzles, battle time-affected enemies, and manipulate time itself to reshape your fate.

    Time-based combat and puzzle mechanics.

    Dynamic environmental changes across different eras.

    Rich narrative with branching story paths based on your decisions.

  • Voidscape

    Genre

    Sci-Fi Roguelike, Exploration

    Platform

    PC / VR

    Description

    Explore the vast, unknown regions of space in Voidscape. As the commander of a lost spaceship, you must navigate through procedurally generated planets, fight hostile alien species, and collect resources to upgrade your ship and survive.

    Infinite procedurally generated worlds.

    Strategic combat with real-time space battles.

    Complex resource management for survival.

  • Eclipse Protocol

    Genre

    Tactical RPG, Espionage

    Platform

    PC / Console

    Description

    Eclipse Protocol is a tactical RPG set in a dystopian future where you lead a covert team of agents on high-stakes espionage missions. Your team’s success relies on strategy, stealth, and resourceful decision-making.

    Turn-based tactical combat with an emphasis on stealth.

    Deep character customization and team building.

    Multiple outcomes based on mission success or failure.
